Membership questions

The things people
ask first.

When does Membership actually launch?

August 2026 — we're using the next few months to run pilot events and refine the recurring experience, with waitlist sign-ups getting first access and founding-member pricing.

Can I still come if I'm not a member?

Yes — Free Tier means no commitment, just $55/ticket for the full event experience. Membership unlocks continuity across events, priority signup, and member-only channels.

What does "matching that remembers you" mean?

At the end of each event you pick people you'd want to see again, and if they pick you back we re-invite both of you to future events — while the ones you didn't click with quietly aren't seated with you again.

What's a facilitator-led event?

A deeper, smaller version of BeHeld with a trained facilitator running the room — reserved for Premium members, available less frequently than standard events.

Can I pause or cancel membership?

Yes — membership is month-to-month, pause or cancel anytime, because you decide the pace.
