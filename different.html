The science behind it

Most events optimize for volume.
We optimize for depth.

01
Shared values
Shared values predict whether a connection lasts — not just whether it sparks. We match on values, life goals, and current life stage before you walk in the door.
02
Repeated contact
Familiarity builds the trust that turns strangers into people you'd actually call. One evening is the introduction. The match system keeps the connection alive.
03
Doing something real together
Shared experience — especially something creative or mildly challenging — creates bonds that conversation alone never can. Our activities surface character, not just conversation.
04
Feeling genuinely seen
Being understood, validated, and cared for is the moment acquaintance becomes connection. Every BeHeld event is designed to create at least one moment where that happens.

The format

Built backwards from how real connection actually forms.

01
You apply. We match on what matters.
Three questions about your values, life goals, and where you are right now. We pair you intentionally — some pairings share your values, some challenge them.
02
Activities that reveal, not just entertain.
Two 30-minute activities, two different partners. Creative and low-pressure — designed to surface more than small talk ever could. Doing together opens people faster than talking about yourself.
03
You learn something every time.
Each event teaches you one skill for showing up better — how to truly witness someone. Not fix them. Not make it about you. Just see them. That's the skill most relationships are missing.
04
Matches informed by what actually happened.
After the event, your next pairing is informed by what you shared. Some matches share your values. Some are chosen to stretch them. Occasionally we'll surprise you — because unexpected connections often teach you the most.
