Upcoming Evenings

Curated gatherings designed for closeness, not contacts — rooms built intentionally, from who's in them to what you do together.

Come as a stranger. Leave having been noticed.

BeHeld creates small, thoughtfully structured gatherings in New York City for people who want more than another evening of polite conversation. Each event uses playful prompts, collaborative games, and guided activities to help people move beyond "What do you do?" and discover how others think, listen, contribute, and show up.

A typical gathering begins with an easy way into the room, followed by activities that gradually create more depth. Our signature Made for You experience invites participants to use what they have noticed to create something for another person, turning attention into a keepsake.

BeHeld is for people seeking meaningful friendships and relationships who are also willing to make the effort those connections require. Attendance is intentionally limited so the room can feel personal, participatory, and cared for.
