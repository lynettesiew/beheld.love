Who this is — and isn't — for

We curate the room. Here's the honest filter.

It's for you if

You've outgrown small talk. You'd rather have one or two real relationships than ten thin ones. You're willing to be a little more honest than usual for an evening.

Common questions

What people usually ask

Is this a dating event or a friendship event?

Both, and neither. It's a connection event. Some people meet partners, some meet close friends, some meet collaborators. We don't sort the room by who's looking for what — we sort it by values and openness, and let the rest happen. The closeness is the goal; the form it takes is up to you.

Why do I have to apply?

Curation does real work. The research on perceived value similarity says rooms with rough value alignment produce dramatically more closeness than randomly assembled ones. We keep groups small and selected so the format can do what it's designed to do.

Can I join The Circle without coming to a single evening first?

Yes. Some people read this and know they want the deeper version. Apply directly via the form — we'll be in touch about the next group.

What if I miss a session in The Circle?

One miss is fine. Two starts to break the familiarity arc for you and the others. We'll talk through it before you commit so you go in with eyes open.

How is this different from group therapy or a workshop?

We're not facilitating healing — we're designing the conditions where genuine connection becomes likely. There's no curriculum, no homework, and no one's working through anything in the room. It's structured the way a great dinner party is structured, just with more intentional design beneath it.
