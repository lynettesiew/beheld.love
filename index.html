The event
What actually happens at a BeHeld event?

You arrive, get settled, and order food or drinks. You'll meet your first partner and do a 30-minute creative activity together — designed to surface more than small talk ever could. A 5-minute reflection follows. Then a second partner, a second activity, another reflection. After both activities you're welcome to stay and mingle.

What kind of activities are these?

Creative, low-pressure, and different every time — drawing, writing, building something. The first Taster uses a dinner-based prompt. Taster 2 is a cooking challenge: two people, one kitchen, 45 minutes, no recipe. What you make and how you make it says more than an hour of conversation.

Is this a dating event?

Not exactly — and not not. We don't pre-sort by what you're looking for. Your people might be a best friend, a partner, a collaborator, or something without a label yet. BeHeld is built for all of them.

Matching
How does the matching work?

Before the event you complete a short application — three questions about your values, life goals, and where you are right now. We pair you intentionally at the event. After the event, your next invitation includes your next pairing informed by what you shared.

What if I want to see someone again?

Tell us after the event. If it's mutual, we'll share details and invite you both to the next event. You don't have to do anything in the room — just let us know afterward.

Why do some pairings have different values?

Because the most interesting connections often come from someone whose world is organised differently than yours. We deliberately include one pairing that challenges your values alongside one that shares them. The difference isn't a mismatch — it's a doorway.

Safety
How do you keep the room safe?

We screen all applications. We're present at every event. Every participant agrees to The BeHeld Code before joining — a short agreement about how we treat each other in the room and after. We take all reports seriously and remove anyone whose behavior threatens the safety or trust of the room.

What if I feel uncomfortable?

Tell us immediately. We're present and paying attention. You can leave at any point — no explanation needed. Being a little uncomfortable in the direction of growth is what we're designing for. Being uncomfortable because of someone else's behavior is not.

Logistics
How much does it cost?

The Taster is $35. This covers your seat and values matching. Food and drinks are ordered separately at the venue.

Why do I have to apply?

Curation does real work. Rooms with value alignment produce dramatically more closeness than randomly assembled ones. We keep groups small and selected — 20 people maximum.

Who typically comes?

Professionals in their 30s and 40s in New York City — software engineers, lawyers, founders, finance professionals, designers, operators. People who are excellent at performing themselves and ready to try something different.
