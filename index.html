Common questions

What people
usually ask.

What actually happens at a BeHeld event?

About two hours in a small, intentional space, moving through structured activities designed to surface character — drawing, storytelling, making, noticing. Less performance, more attention. Stay and mingle after if you'd like.

Is this a dating event?

Not exactly, and not not. We don't pre-sort by what you're looking for, because your people might be a best friend, a partner, a collaborator, or something without a label yet.

How does the matching work?

A short application before the event shapes who you'll be sitting with, and after the event your next invitation is informed by who you connected with — mutual matches come back, the ones you didn't click with quietly don't.

What if I want to see someone again?

Tell us at the end of the event, and if they pick you back we'll let you both know — then you choose what happens next, either a direct introduction or a reserved spot at the next event together.

How much does it cost?

Single events are $55, which covers your seat and matching. Recurring monthly membership and premium tiers are launching August 2026.

How do you keep the room safe?

Every participant agrees to The BeHeld Code — a short agreement about how we treat each other, with serious consequences for anyone whose behavior threatens the trust of the room.
