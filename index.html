A real fit

Who this is — and isn't — for

We curate the room. Here's the honest filter.

It's for you if

You've outgrown small talk. You'd rather have one or two real relationships than ten thin ones. You're willing to be a little more honest than usual for an evening.

It's not for you if

You want a packed room or a numbers game. You'd rather optimize for volume than depth. You're not willing to have one slightly vulnerable conversation.

Common questions

What people usually ask

How does the matching work?

After a Single Evening, you tell us who you'd like to know more. If they say yes back, it's a match. We share their profile with you and yours with them, and invite you both into what comes next — another evening together in the next curated room, or The Circle.

How do you keep the room safe?

We curate the room. We screen applications. We're present at every event. Every participant agrees to The BeHeld Code before joining — a short agreement about how we treat each other in the room and after. We take all reports of inappropriate behavior seriously, and we remove anyone whose behavior threatens the safety or trust of the room. The Circle has explicit exits at any session — both parties can stop, no questions asked.

What if my match doesn't opt into The Circle?

Both of you have to opt in for The Circle to start. If only you opt in, you can choose to be re-paired with someone else who's opted in — or join an existing pod of two or more. Minimum two participants to begin.

Is this a dating event or a friendship event?

Both, and neither. We don't pre-sort by what you're looking for — we sort by values and openness, and let the connection take whatever shape it's going to take. Some matches become romantic. Some become close friendships. Some become creative collaborations. The format doesn't presume.

Why do I have to apply?

Curation does real work. Rooms with rough value alignment produce dramatically more closeness than randomly assembled ones. We keep groups small and selected so the format can do what it's designed to do.

Can I join The Circle without coming to a single evening first?

Yes. Some people read this and know they want the deeper version. Apply directly via the form — we'll be in touch about the next group.

What if I miss a session in The Circle?

One miss is fine. Two starts to break the arc for you and the others. We'll talk through it before you commit so you go in with eyes open.

How is this different from group therapy or a workshop?

We're not facilitating healing — we're designing the conditions where genuine connection becomes likely. There's no curriculum, no homework, and no one's working through anything in the room. It's structured the way a great dinner party is structured, just with more intentional design beneath it.

One last thing

Come once. See how it feels.

If it doesn't land, you've spent one evening. If it does, it might be the most useful Tuesday of your year.
