Our story

Built for the loneliness
nobody talks about.

The people who struggle most with loneliness aren't the ones without social skills. They're the ones surrounded by people — and still feeling unknown.

Why we built this

Connection isn't something you find.
It's something you build.

BeHeld was born out of a personal experience of moving to a new city, building a full life on paper — career, friends, a calendar — and still feeling like nobody really knew me.

The apps weren't built for this. The events weren't built for this. Speed dating optimises for efficiency. Networking events optimise for performance. Neither optimises for the thing that actually matters: being truly known by someone.

"Feeling seen doesn't require sameness. It requires attention."

BeHeld is built around what the research actually says about how humans form genuine bonds — and designed so that every element serves that one goal.
