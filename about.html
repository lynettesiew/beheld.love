Our story

Built for the loneliness
nobody talks about.

The people who struggle most with loneliness aren't the ones without social skills, but the ones surrounded by people and still feeling unknown.

Why we built this

Connection isn't something you find,
it's something you build.

I moved to a new city and built a full life on paper — career, friends, a calendar — and still felt like nobody really knew me.

"The best relationships were never chosen from a menu."

You meet people at school, at work, through someone — places where you keep showing up, and what each relationship becomes reveals itself over time. BeHeld is built on that older logic.

The founder
Lynette

Product manager by day, community builder by instinct. I moved to New York at 23 and built my life from scratch — BeHeld is the thing I wish had existed.
